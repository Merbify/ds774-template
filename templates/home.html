Welcome!

Thank you for visiting our website! We are a local business run by immagrants, trying to give others the experiences of an authentic arab culture. Stop by anytime Monday - Saturday from 9-5 and feel free to ask for a tour! We'd be pleased to show you around.

Here is a little bit about us: Liban D'or opened up in Methuen, MA in 2010. It's run by the Eli and Lina Merheb with the support of their three sons. It provides the products found in any lebanese childhood here in the United States. From fresh pita bread, to authentic Lebanese wine / hard alcohol. Fresh lebanese cuisine can also be ordered daily such as homemade Hummus, Zaatar, and even baklava.
